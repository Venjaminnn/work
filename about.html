Я начинающий дизайнер с большим потенциалом и огромным желанием узнавать новое.

from designing physical spaces to digital ones

I’m currently a M.S. Integrated Product Design candidate at the University of Pennsylvania. It is a joint degree between the Weitzman School of Design, Wharton School of Business, and School of Engineering and Applied Sciences.

I have a traditional design education, with a B.A. in Architecture from the University of California, Berkeley on a merit scholarship. I also extended my knowledge in architecture and design in Singapore for a semester.

Architecture taught me how to manage complexity at various scales, use abstraction for design mediation, and understand diverse priorities and perspectives.

Now, I’m channeling those skills into interface and interaction design. I’m passionate about crafting intuitive and innovative experiences that allow users to navigate (digital and/or physical) space seamlessly.
